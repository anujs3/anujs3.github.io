Places I've Worked

HackUCI

HackUCI is a 3-day hackathon at UC Irvine where hundreds of college students come together to develop software from scratch to win prizes. As the Director of Corporate Outreach, I am responsible for contacting sponsors to attend our annual event. In November 2015, we hosted our second hackathon at the eSports Arena in Santa Ana, California. Read more about it here!

Sonder VR

Sonder VR is an application that helps people with Autism Spectrum Disorder learn and practice their social skills. I helped develop the application in Unity and create the business case for the product. Our application uses virtual reality to teach kids with autism about body language. We had participated in the Butterworth Development Competition at UCI and won first place!

Space Gods

Space Gods is a 2D arena-based fighting game where you battle gods from different universes. For this game project, I collaborated with 10+ developers. I was in charge of programming the game and creating the sound effects. Our game is available for download on PC and Mac. Check it out!
